Why teams put this in front of their AI traffic

Before
  • A different SDK per provider
  • A key to leak per provider
  • No shared view of what's being spent, or where
  • Someone pages you when a provider degrades
  • Cost tracking lives in five different dashboards
After
  • One OpenAI-compatible endpoint
  • One scoped, revocable virtual key
  • Every request logged, traced, and billed
  • Circuit breakers fail over automatically
  • One ledger, one price table, one currency view

What that actually buys you
